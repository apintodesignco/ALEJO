Biometric Data Consent

This demo will use your camera to track your eyes for calibration purposes. All processing is done locally in your browser, and no data is sent to any server.

The following data will be collected:

  • Eye position and movement
  • Gaze direction
  • Calibration accuracy metrics

You can revoke consent at any time by clicking the "Stop" button.
