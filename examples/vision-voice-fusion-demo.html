ALEJO Vision-Voice Fusion Demo

About This Demo

This demo showcases ALEJO's multimodal fusion capabilities, combining vision and voice inputs for enhanced understanding of user intent and emotional state.

How it works: The system will analyze your facial expressions through the camera and your voice through the microphone. It will then combine these inputs to provide a more accurate understanding of your emotional state and enhance command recognition.

Privacy note: All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server or stored permanently.
